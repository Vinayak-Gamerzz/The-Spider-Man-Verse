Year 2002

Spider Man

Key Details

Release Date: 3 May 2002

Director: Sam Raimi

Writer: David Koepp

Distributor: Columbia Pictures (Sony Pictures)

Box Office: Made over $821 Million worldwide, breaking records at the time.

Cinematic First: The first film in Hollywood history to cross $100 Million in a single weekend.

Major Comic Deviations: This version featured organic web-shooters from Peter's wrists rather than mechanical web-shooters constructed by Peter.

Main Cast

  • Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker / Spider-Man
  • Willem Dafoe as Norman Osborn / Green Goblin
  • Kirsten Dunst as Mary Jane Watson
  • James Franco as Harry Osborn
  • J.K. Simmons as J. Jonah Jameson
  • Rosemary Harris as Aunt May
  • Cliff Robertson as Uncle Ben

Story Summary

The Origin:

Nerdy teen Peter Parker is bitten by a genetically engineered "Super-Spider". He gains Incredible strength, wall-crwaling abilities, and organic wrist web-shooters

The Villian:

Meanwhile, industrialist Norman Osborn tests an unstable military serum on himself. It drives him insane, turning him into the high-tech, flying Green Goblin.

The Tragedy:

Out of spite, Peter lets a thief escape. That same thief later murders his Uncle Ben. Devastated by guilt, Peter adopts his uncle's motto "With great power comes great responsibility" and becomes Spider-Man.

The Climax:

Norman learns Peter's identity and kidnaps Peter's crush, MJ. Spider-Man saves her and defeats the Goblin in a brutal final battle.

The Ending:

Norman is accidentally killed by his own flying glider, His son, Harry blames Spide-Man and vows revenge. To keep MJ safe from future enemies, Peter hides his feelings and chooses a lonely life as a hero.

Year 2004

Spider-Man 2

Key Details

Release Date: 30 June, 2004

Director: Sam Raimi

Screen Writer: Alvin Sargent

Distributor: Columbia Pictures (Sony Pictures)

Box Office: Grossed over $788 Million worldwide

Major Accolade: Won the Academy Award for Best Visual Effects

Main Cast

Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker / Spider-Man

Alfred Molina as Dr. Octto Octavius / Doctor Octopus (Doc Ock)

Kirsten Dunst as Mary Jane Watson (MJ)

James Franco as Harry Osborn

Rosemary Harris as Aunt May

J.K. Simmons as J. Jonah Jameson

Story Summary

The Struggle:

Two years later, Peter's personal life is a disaster. He is broke, failing college, losing his love Mary-Jane to an engagement, and his best friend Harry wants Spider-Man dead.

Powers Lost:

The intense stress manifests physically, causing Peter to lose his superpowers. Exhausted, he throws his suit in the trash and decides to live a normal life
